# Vakratund-Store-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard Creation Using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize sales performance, customer behavior, and order trends of Vakratund Store using an interactive dashboard. The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making. Overall, the project helps in improving business strategies, increasing sales efficiency, and enhancing customer targeting through data insights.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/anilsakhare-gthb/data-analysis-dashboard/blob/main/vakratund-store-analysis.csv">Vakratund Store Dataset</a>

## Questions (Dashboard Requirements)
- In which month are both sales amount and order count at their peak.
- Is there any month where sales increased but order count decreased (or vice versa)? What could be the reason.
- Which gender contributes more to total sales, and what is their percentage share.
- What is the combined percentage of problematic orders (Cancelled + Returned + Refunded).
- Which state generates the highest sales, and how does it compare to the lowest among the top 5 states.
- Which age group has the highest order contribution, and how does it differ between men and women.
- Which sales channel contributes the highest share of orders.
- What is the percentage difference between the top two sales channels.
- Dashboard Interaction- <a href="https://github.com/anilsakhare-gthb/data-analysis-dashboard/blob/main/firstdashbd.jpg">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Collect and understand the dataset to identify key business requirements.
- Clean and prepare the data to ensure accuracy and consistency.
- Create important KPIs and metrics for performance tracking.
- Design and build interactive dashboard visuals for better understanding.
- Analyze insights and trends to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dashboard Insights
- Female customers contribute approximately 60–65% of total sales, while male customers account for around 35–40%, indicating a strong female customer base.
- Nearly 90–92% of total orders are successfully delivered, whereas 8–10% fall under cancellations, returns, or refunds, showing good but improvable fulfillment performance.
- The top 5 states contribute around 50–60% of total revenue, with the highest single state contributing nearly 15–20% alone.
- Adults (age group 25–45) generate about 60–70% of total orders, making them the primary target customer segment.
- The leading sales channel contributes approximately 35–40% of total orders, while the second-best channel contributes around 20–25%, showing platform dependency.

## Tools Used
- Data Analysis: Excel
- Visualization: Excel
- Documentation: Markdown

## Conclusion
The Vakratund Store Dashboard clearly shows that the business is performing well, with strong sales trends and a high percentage of successful deliveries. Female customers and the adult age group contribute the most, making them the key target audience.

Sales are mainly driven by a few top states and major online channels, which is good but also shows some dependency. At the same time, a small number of returns and cancellations indicate there is still room for improvement.

Overall, the dashboard makes it easy to understand business performance and highlights where to focus for future growth and better results.
